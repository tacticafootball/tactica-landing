Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14/07/2026

tactica. ("we," "us," "our") is operated by Peter Scarratt, trading as tactica. (15 The Malthouse, Taunton, TA1 1RU). This policy explains what personal data we collect through tacticafootball.co.uk, why, and what rights you have over it.

What we collect

Email address — if you join our early access waitlist, we collect the email address you provide.

Analytics data — if you consent to analytics cookies, we collect information about how you use this site via Google Analytics, including pages visited, approximate location (derived from IP address), device and browser type, and how you arrived at the site.

We do not collect payment information, and we do not currently require an account to use this site.

Why we collect it

  • Email address: to send you updates about tactica.'s development and launch, and, if you request it, an invite to our Discord community. We do not use your email for any other purpose, and we do not sell or share it with third parties for marketing purposes.
  • Analytics data: to understand how people use the site and to improve it. This data is aggregated and does not identify you personally.

Legal basis

Under UK GDPR, we rely on:

  • Consent — for analytics cookies (you can accept or decline these; declining doesn't affect your ability to use the site) and for joining the waitlist (you actively submit your email).

Who we share data with

  • Mailchimp (The Rocket Science Group LLC, USA) — processes and stores waitlist email addresses on our behalf.
  • Google Analytics (Google LLC, USA) — processes anonymised/aggregated usage data, only if you accept analytics cookies.

Both are based in the United States.

How long we keep data

  • Waitlist email addresses are kept until tactica. launches publicly or until you unsubscribe, whichever is sooner. You can unsubscribe at any time via the link in any email we send, or by contacting us directly.
  • Analytics data is retained according to Google Analytics' standard retention settings (Event Data Retention 2 Months / User Data 14 Months).

Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict how we process your data
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent at any time (this won't affect processing that already happened while consent was given)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@tacticafootball.co.uk.

Cookies

See our cookie banner for details on what cookies this site uses and to change your preference at any time. In short: we only use cookies for analytics (Google Analytics), and only after you've actively accepted them. No advertising or marketing cookies are currently used.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as tactica. develops. Material changes will be reflected with an updated "Last updated" date above.

Contact

Questions about this policy or your data: hello@tacticafootball.co.uk
